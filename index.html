What I do

I clarify how work moves through your organization (intake, handoffs, coordination, follow-through), then automate the right parts using modern AI and integration tools.

The goal is reliability and understanding, not hacks.

Example workflows

  • Client or candidate intake and triage
  • Internal coordination and handoffs
  • Purchasing and approvals
  • Content creation and reuse
  • Reporting, summaries, and follow-ups

A different approach to automation

Automation projects often fail when tools are added before workflows are clear. Clearform starts with system design so AI supports people instead of creating fragility.
