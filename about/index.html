About

Clearform is a small consulting practice focused on improving how work moves through service-based organizations. The emphasis is on clarity, durability, and human ownership.

What “workflow redesign” means

Most teams have tools, but the work between tools is where things break: intake, handoffs, decision points, coordination, and follow-through.

Clearform maps those steps, reduces unnecessary friction, and then automates the right parts. The point is not maximum automation. It’s a system people can understand and trust.
